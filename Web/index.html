Guitarfroggy is a website dedicated to encouraging beginner to pro guitarists to learn and practice guitar. With a collection of free resources to learn and practice your skills from, to short lessons about guitar basics and the fundamentals of the guitar, guitarfroggy strives to encourage many to learn the simple but wonderful aspect of guitar! We quote Radiohead's song that 'Anyone can play guitar'. We really believe everyone can, with enough determination!
